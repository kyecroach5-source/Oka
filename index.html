What is OKA's Okra?

OKA's okra is dry powder okra from finely ground okra, harvested at peak freshness and dehydrated to preserve its natural nutrients. Itâ€™s convenient, shelf-stable, and easy to incorporate into everyday meals.

How is it Made?
  • Selection: Fresh, tender okra pods are carefully chosen.
  • Washing & Slicing: Pods are cleaned and sliced thinly.
  • Drying: Slices are sun-dried or dehydrated at low temperature to retain nutrients.
  • Grinding: Fully dried slices are ground into fine powder.
  • Packaging: Stored in airtight containers to maintain freshness.
Uses
  • Mix into smoothies or juices for a fiber-rich boost.
  • Add to soups, stews, or sauces for thickening and nutrition.
  • Sprinkle over cereals, rice, or salads.
  • Use as a natural supplement in baked goods or snacks.
Health Benefits
  • Rich in Fiber: Supports digestion and gut health.
  • Packed with Vitamins & Minerals: Contains vitamin C, K, magnesium, and antioxidants.
  • Blood Sugar Support: May help regulate glucose levels.
  • Heart & Kidney Health: Promotes healthy cholesterol and kidney function.
  • Weight Management: Low-calorie, nutrient-dense addition to meals.
Why You Should Use It

    Dry powder okra combines convenience, versatility, and health in one natural product. It lasts longer than fresh okra without refrigeration. Just a spoonful a day can boost nutrient intake, aid digestion, and support overall wellness naturally.
